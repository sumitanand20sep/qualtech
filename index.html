Let's assume that you are 30 years old today and a non-smoker and you have chosen this option with policyterm of 30 years.

You pay a premium of ₹ 1 Lac every year for the next 12 years

When you turn 42 years old, you would have paid premium of ₹ 12 lacs

However, in this plan during your age span between 43 to 53 years, ie for the next 11 years, you get ₹ 1.5 lacs every year
